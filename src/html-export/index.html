Understanding Panchakarma

Panchakarma is a comprehensive detoxification and rejuvenation program from Ayurveda. These five therapeutic procedures work together to eliminate toxins, restore balance, and promote optimal health through natural healing methods.

Vaman

Therapeutic Vomiting

A cleansing therapy that eliminates toxins from the upper respiratory tract and stomach.

Key Benefits:

  • Removes excess Kapha
  • Clears respiratory congestion
  • Improves digestion
Learn More

Virechan

Purgation Therapy

A purification process that cleanses the small intestine and liver through controlled purgation.

Key Benefits:

  • Eliminates excess Pitta
  • Detoxifies liver
  • Improves skin health
Learn More

Basti

Medicated Enema

A therapeutic enema using herbal decoctions to cleanse and nourish the colon.

Key Benefits:

  • Balances Vata dosha
  • Strengthens nervous system
  • Improves joint health
Learn More

Nasya

Nasal Therapy

Administration of medicated oils through the nasal passages to treat head and neck disorders.

Key Benefits:

  • Clears sinuses
  • Improves mental clarity
  • Treats headaches
Learn More

Raktamokshana

Bloodletting Therapy

A purification therapy that removes impure blood to treat various skin and blood disorders.

Key Benefits:

  • Purifies blood
  • Treats skin disorders
  • Reduces inflammation
Learn More
