Authentic Panchakarma Treatments

Discover the five sacred therapies of Ayurveda designed to detoxify, rejuvenate, and restore your body's natural balance. Each treatment is carefully administered by certified practitioners using traditional methods and authentic herbs.

The Five Pillars of Panchakarma

Panchakarma, meaning "five actions," represents the most comprehensive detoxification and rejuvenation program in Ayurveda. These time-tested therapies work synergistically to eliminate toxins, balance doshas, and restore optimal health.

7-14 Days

Vaman

Therapeutic Vomiting

A controlled therapeutic vomiting procedure that eliminates excess Kapha dosha and toxins from the upper respiratory tract and stomach. Highly effective for respiratory and digestive disorders.

Key Benefits:

  • Removes excess Kapha dosha
  • Clears respiratory congestion
  • Improves digestion and metabolism
  • Treats chronic cough and asthma

10-15 Days

Virechan

Purgation Therapy

A controlled purgation therapy that cleanses the small intestine, liver, and eliminates excess Pitta dosha. Excellent for liver disorders and inflammatory conditions.

Key Benefits:

  • Eliminates excess Pitta dosha
  • Detoxifies liver and gallbladder
  • Improves skin health and complexion
  • Treats inflammatory conditions

15-30 Days

Basti

Medicated Enema

The most important Panchakarma therapy involving medicated enemas to cleanse and nourish the colon. Highly effective for Vata disorders and neurological conditions.

Key Benefits:

  • Balances Vata dosha effectively
  • Strengthens nervous system
  • Improves joint health and mobility
  • Enhances reproductive health
Learn More
7-21 Days

Nasya

Nasal Therapy

Administration of medicated oils through nasal passages to treat head and neck disorders. Excellent for sinus problems, headaches, and mental clarity.

Key Benefits:

  • Clears sinuses and nasal passages
  • Improves mental clarity
  • Treats chronic headaches
  • Enhances sensory functions

1-7 Days

Raktamokshana

Bloodletting Therapy

A specialized therapy involving controlled removal of impure blood to treat blood-related disorders. Highly effective for skin conditions and inflammatory diseases.

Key Benefits:

  • Purifies blood and circulation
  • Treats various skin disorders
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Manages hypertension naturally
